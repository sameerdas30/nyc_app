NYC Royal Limo has been the premiere NY limousine service in the Tri State Area since 2001. We offer a complete service with our extensive fleet of Sedans, SUV’s, Super Stretch and Buses. Whatever you need and size of your party we can offer the finest accommodations.

As a full service limousine company we offer deluxe accommodations for every facet of the industry. Some of the services we provide are:

• Wedding Service – We provide the ultimate in luxury for the bride, groom, family and bridal party with super stretch limos complete with light shows, sound systems, wet bars and more. Tinted windows insure privacy. With us, the reception begins on the way and continues after you arrive.
• Airport Transportation - The finest accommodations are available around the clock, seven days per week to La Guardia, Kennedy, Newark Liberty, Teterboro, Mac Arthur and Westchester. Our drivers are the best at knowing the ins and outs of all the terminals and surrounding roadways.
• NY City Tours – Treat yourself or your guests to a guided tour of New York with a city limo tour. Our specially trained drivers will provide more than just the tourist spots. You will learn about history and infamy and the neighborhoods that make New York what it is today.
• Sports Venues or a Night on the Town – Let us do the driving and you have the fun. Your party will be safe and you will not have to worry about driving or parking.
• Bachelor or Bachelorette Party – Have the time of your life from the safety and luxury of a stretch Hummer or other vehicle. Pull out all the stops and the champagne while we assure your safe return home.

At NYC Royal Limo we strive to be the best and remain the best. Our drivers are experienced licensed chauffeurs who undergo background checks and drug testing. They are required to maintain a clean driving record so you need not be concerned. Your complete satisfaction with our service and your safety are our two highest priorities and we are sure your experience will affirm that fact.

We serve the Manhattan, Brooklyn, Bronx, Queens, Staten Island, New Jersey, Rockland and Orange Counties, Westchester and Southwestern Ct. Other cities are served by our affiliates nationwide.
